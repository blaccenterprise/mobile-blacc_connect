California's B.L.A.C.C. Connect Memo

B.L.A.C.C. Connect is hosted on a private Linux server created by Stevie W. Carpenter using Node.js on GoogleChrome V8 Javascript Engine. Files initiated in the recent third party server (w3spaces) will be securely transferred to the private server by the end of the first quarter of 2025. The current counties that can be navigated are Los Angeles, Kern and San Bernardino. Los Angeles county has 5 districts with a total of 88 outlined cities with detailed resources. Kern County has 4 districts with a total of 35 cities with detailed reources. San Bernardino is beiing outlined and within the developmental stage, cities outlined will be presented in full by the end of the fourth quarter of 2024.
