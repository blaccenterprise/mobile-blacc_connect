WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files containing letters and numbers that are sent by a webster (such as the www.newnexxus.org webster) to your computer, smartphone, tablet, or any other device with internet access which you use to visit our Website (“Device”). Once a cookie is stored on your Device, the Website server may be able to recognize the Device you use.

“Web Beacons” are small graphic data (also known as ‘Pixel Tags’ or ‘Clear GIFs’), which can be included on our Website and newsletters and are usually used in combination with cookies to identify users and user behavior.

B.L.A.C.C. Corporation uses different types of cookies on the Website. These cookies serve different purposes. According to their purpose, cookies may generally be assigned to one of the following categories:

  •

WILL COOKIES BE PERMANENTLY STORED ON MY DEVICE?
                                                         

                                                   

Whether and for how long cookies remain stored on a Device mainly depends on whether the cookie Created a Persistent Cookie or a Session Cookie. “Session Cookies” will remain stored on your Device only until you close the browser. By contrast, “Persistent Cookies” will remain stored on your Device until they expire or are deleted by you (e.g., by deleting your browsing history or by your targeted deletion of the cookies via your browser settings). For the duration of storage of the particular cookies, please see the Cookie Table below.
